## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Size**: 512x512px (square)
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Style**: Product photography or clean renders

## Brands Featured

- **Thrasher** - Iconic skate magazine brand
- **Baker** - Andrew Reynolds' skate company
- **Piss Drunk (PD)** - Skate brand
- **Hours is Yours** - Skate brand
- **Shake Junt** - Trick accessory brand

- **Thunder** - Truck manufacturer

## Getting Images

1. **Download from brand websites** (check licensing)
2. **Use placeholder images** temporarily
3. **Create custom renders** for unique items

## Placeholder Until Real Assets

The app will show a broken image icon if these files are missing. Add placeholder images or real product photos to make the closet functional.
